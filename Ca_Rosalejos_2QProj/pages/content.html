About Me
why mental health awareness?
I chose mental health awareness because I aspire to be able to help people understand themselves deeper. In a third-world country like the Philippines, a lot of people still brush off mental health as an unnecessary thing, I want to get rid of this mentality in Filipinos for the good of the new generation. As someone who has wanted help but didn’t know where to seek it out, I want to let as many people as possible know that they in fact, not alone in this and that they can ask for help anytime. Being able to help people gives me genuine happiness and peace, even for just a short while.
