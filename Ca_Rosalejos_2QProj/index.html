mental health awareness
this is the ongoing effort to reduce the stigma around mental illness and mental health conditions by sharing personal experiences
a timely reminder that mental health is essential and that those living with mental health issues are deserving of care, understanding, compassion, and pathways to hope, healing, recovery, and fulfillment.
a brief history
Mental health month was established in 1949 to increase awareness of the importance of mental health in American’s lives and to celebrate recovery from mental illnesses, for mental health is essential for a person’s overall health.
